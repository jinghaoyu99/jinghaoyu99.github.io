About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Department of Mathematical Sciences, Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. My advisor is Zhengyu Zong.

From 2017 to 2021, I was an undergraduate student at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China. I received my bachelor's at SUSTech in 2021.

Research Interests

Gromov-Witten Theory

The Gromov-Witten theory is a virtual counting of curves on a manifold (orbifold).

Topological Recursion

A recursion algorithm initiated from random matrix theory.
